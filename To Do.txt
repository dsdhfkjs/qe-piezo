-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
						Piezoelectricity Flowchart (Shi 2014)
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Procedure
1. Optimize structure until forces are less than 5 meV/Ang
2. Vary strain along [001] direction from -0.5% to +0.5%
3. Optimize perpendicular lattice vectors and internal atomic positions until stresses (sigma_11, sigma_22) are smaller than 0.01 GPa
4. Calculate polarization via Berry phase
5. Use a finite difference method to get d_33 = dP/dsigma_3
